I am a CS PhD student at the [University of Maryland](https://cs.umd.edu),
advised by [Prof. Haizhao Yang](https://haizhaoyang.github.io/). I am interested
in AI and data-driven ML methods applied to inverse problems, in particular inverse
PDEs. My research focuses on data-efficient, parameter-efficient, and
compute-efficient methods for accelerating scientific computing — including
curriculum learning and agentic workflows for scientific applications.

I am also a full-time Machine Learning Engineer at [Scale AI](https://scale.ai).

Previously, I was a Mathematics PhD student (2022–2024) at UMD, where I received
an M.A. in 2024. My master's work explored the intersection of algebraic geometry
and homotopy theory through the lens of motivic homotopy theory and synthetic
spectra. That work is available in my
[master's thesis](https://doi.org/10.13016/as9f-km0b).
